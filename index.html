BANK

You and your opponent are bidding for properties in Seattle! In each round of the game, a random property will be displayed, and you will each place a secret bid into an auction for the property. Whoever bids more will win the auction.

Once a player has won, the property's true value will be shown. The winning player's score for that round is equal to how much he/she under or overpaid for the item.

Use your estimation skills as well as the data provided for each property to appraise its value. You have to offer more than your opponent to win a property. But don't bid too much! If you offer more than the home is worth you will lose money in your bank account. Happy hunting!
